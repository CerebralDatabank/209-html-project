A as in Aggies

What is A as in Aggies?

Where does it take place?

Local Schools within a 30 mile radius

What time does it take place?

The Friday before Spring Break: Between 8am and 2pm

Who can Particpate?

Everyone who has a GPA of at least 2.0

A as in Aggies is an university wide volunteer event that takes place the Friday before spring break. In advance Aggies sign up and are assigned to a high school where they volunteer for the day. From painting murals on the side of buildings to reading with kindergarten students, Aggies spent the day working with kids and assisting in any way the school really needs and students are able. Students who are eligible and choose to participate are then receive attendance credit for all their classes and an A on any assignments due that day that are turned in. This volunteer opportunity exemplifies the values of Rellis, Respect for their community and future students, Excellence in the classroom and in daily lives, Leadership towards those kids they are working with, Loyalty to their community, and Integrity in being honest about where they are spending their time that day.
